Massuni

Custom Furniture

Massuni was a project that aimed to change the way people order furniture. Users would select a piece of furniture they like, and then transform everything from the material, stain, size, and functionality of the piece to suit their unique space. They'd be able to view a 3D rendering of the furniture throughout the design process.

Technology Used: Python, Django, Cython, Angular, RabbitMQ, Redis, Django Celery, WebGL, Postgresql, SASS, jQuery/Javascript, RQueue, Reality Server

ACM

Order Forms

ACM was seeking a way to simplify product orders for a corporate client with over 600 retail stores in Canada. Previously stores had placed orders either through email, phone call, or even using a fax order form. To modernize things the goal was to move the process online by developing simple and easy-to-use web forms.

Technology Used: Python, Django, ReactJS, Postgresql, SASS, jQuery/Javascript
